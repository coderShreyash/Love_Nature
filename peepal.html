Peepal Tree

Description:

  1. Peepal is a large, fast growing deciduous tree.
  2. It has a heart shaped leaves.
  3. It is a medium size tree and has a large crown with the wonderful wide spreading branches.
  4. It shed its leaves in the month of March and April.
  5. The fruits of the Peepal are hidden with the figs.
  6. The figs are ripen in the month of May.
  7. The figs which contain the flowers grow in pairs just below the leaves and look like the berries.
  8. Its bark is light gray and peels in patches.
  9. Its fruit is purple in colour.
  10. It is one of the longest living trees.

Location:

  1. Peepal tree is grown throughout India.
  2. It is mainly grown in State of Haryana, Bihar, Kerala and Madhya Pradesh.
  3. It is also found in the Ranthambore National Park in India.
