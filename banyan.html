Banyan Tree

Description:

  1. Banyan tree is a huge tree with very extensive branches.
  2. It is said that at one time more than 10, 000 people can sit under its shade at one time.
  3. It is a evergreen tree, i.e, it never sheds leaves.
  4. It branches spread out and send trunk like roots to the ground in order to support itself.
  5. It grows to a height of more than 21 meters and lives for many years.
  6. The leaves are 10 - 20 cm long and has many aerial roots.
  7. The leaves are broad, oval and glossy, White milky fluid oozes out of leaves, if broken.
  8. It can grow in to the gaint tree covering several hectares.

Location:

  1. Found in almost all the parts of India, Banyan tree is the National tree of India.
  2. It is grown throughout the sub- Himalayan region and in the deciduous forests.
  3. One can Banyan Tree in the Botanical Garden of Calcutta.
  4. They are widely grown in the Ranthambore National Park and Corbett National Park in India.
